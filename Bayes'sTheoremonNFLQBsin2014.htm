Bayes'sTheoremonNFLQBsin2014 at LEARNINGlover.com

This script looks at QB stats from every game in the the 2014 NFL year and uses Bayes's Theorem to determine the conditional probability of victory given a statistics in the categories (Completions, Attempts, Completion %, Yards, Touchdowns, Interceptions, and Rating). Use the select box to see the category you want to consider and it will look at all the values in that category from last year (I decided to put them into ranges to make the categories discrete and easy to navigate).
