Math Definitions Program

When I learned what math really was, I changed the way I took notes in math classes. Instead of focusing so much on numbers and formulas, I spent a lot more of my time focusing on definitions and theorems. This program allows users to test their knowledge of math definitions by providing a list of terms and definitions and giving users a matching game where the objective is to match each term with its corresponding definition. Users will click on a term on the left and a definition on the right (that corresponds to the term) and press submit. If the term and definition match, then the two will be removed from the set. The user should continue until all terms and definitions have been matched.
